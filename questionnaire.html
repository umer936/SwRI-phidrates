Atomic & Molecular Database Questionnaire

We have started work on an online database for basic atomic and molecular data. The first phase of the database contains cross sections for dissociation, ionization, and photodissociative ionization. We calculate rate coefficients (inverse lifetimes) using the solar spectrum as a function of solar activity. Future calculations will include rate coefficients using blackbody spectra with temperature as input, and an interstellar radiation spectrum. We calculate the excess energies of the ionization or dissociation products and provide plots of cross sections, rate coefficients, and excess energies as a function of wavelength. Many results can be downloaded for use on your computer. In the future, effects of optical depth will be included for some planetary and satellite atmospheres.
